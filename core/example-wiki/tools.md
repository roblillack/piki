# Toolbelt

## Daily Drivers

- **Slack** – Team comms, incident channel, async updates
- **Linear** – Track sprint work, backlog grooming, roadmap visibility
- **Notion** – Shared knowledge base for policies and onboarding
- **VS Code** – Primary editor with Rust Analyzer + GitLens
- **Warp** – Terminal with command recall + workflows

## Analytics & Reporting

- **Metabase** – Quick dashboards for finance snapshots
- **Looker Studio** – Executive reporting with scheduled email exports
- **dbt Cloud** – Transformations and lineage tracking

## Personal Productivity

- **Fantastical** – Calendar triage, conflict spotting
- **FocusGuard** – Pomodoro timer + website blocker
- **Strava** – Syncs marathon training plan and heart rate trends

## Wish List / To Evaluate

- **Height** for cross-team planning (potential replacement for Linear)
- **Raycast** for faster macOS app launching + clipboard history
- **Cron** for better multi-time-zone scheduling
